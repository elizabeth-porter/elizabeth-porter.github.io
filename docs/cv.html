Exhibitions Curated

June - August 2025 Manufactured Ecosystems
Co-curator with collaborators Dowhaniuk, D., Ponte, A., Yu, A., Hiemstra, H., Jandrew, S.
Zavitz Gallery, University of Guelph. Guelph, ON.

  • An immersive exhibition exploring speculative futures at the intersection of art, technology, and nature to address the climate crisis. Served as the public-facing centrepiece of an international research initiative supported by the Government of Canada’s New Frontiers in Research Fund. Featured interactive installations, speculative prototypes, and imaginative storytelling envisioning how humanity might adapt to ecological collapse through nature-inspired technologies.
